# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary user is a creator preparing a sponsor-facing YouTube workflow demo for Pollo AI. Secondary users are reviewers or collaborators who need to understand the path from short-form trend research to usable AI video ad directions.

## Product Purpose

This tool is a local Instagram Reels scout for a Pollo AI / Seedance 2.0 Ultra video. It helps find repeatable short-form hooks, formats, visual styles, and product angles, then turns selected examples into prompt directions for UGC-style clips, product promos, reviews, unboxings, explainers, and agency campaign variants.

Success means the creator can follow one happy path on camera: search Reels, identify strong hooks, star examples, copy a Pollo-ready prompt, open Pollo AI, and generate several Seedance 2.0 directions quickly.

## Brand Personality

Practical, sponsor-ready, workflow-first. The app should feel like a focused production tool, not a generic social dashboard or a broad AI model playground.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic model-demo UI, marketing landing-page composition, decorative AI gradients, and loose research surfaces that do not lead to a clear next action. Do not make Ultra Plan details feel like a separate feature list; connect them to the workflow.

## Design Principles

- Start with Pollo AI and Seedance 2.0 early so the sponsor value is visible immediately.
- Keep the flow concrete: trend research, shortlist, prompt, generate, compare outputs.
- Optimize for an 8-10 minute screen-recording happy path with few detours.
- Make creative volume and iteration feel practical, not theoretical.
- Keep the tool dense and calm enough to use live while recording.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable labels, visible focus states where possible, strong contrast on controls, and avoid relying only on color to communicate state.
